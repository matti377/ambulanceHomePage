The CPR timeline, captured as it happens.

CPR Logger is a bedside-first resuscitation logger for the seconds that matter. Start with the initial rhythm, record shocks and medications in one tap, switch into post-ROSC reassessment, and export a clean PDF handover without sending the session off the device.

From first compression to handover

Four steps. Minimal friction. A clean record at the end.

1

Choose the rhythm

Start from pVT, VF, PEA, asystole, or unknown and launch the session clock immediately.

2

Log the events

Tap shocks, medications, and interventions while the timers keep the cycle visible.

3

Record ROSC

Switch into the post-ROSC ABCDE workflow and capture the reassessment in structure, not scraps.

4

Export and share

Create the PDF event log and hand over a readable record to the next team.
